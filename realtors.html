How We Work

Step 1: Lead Acquisition

We source leads from over 150 platforms and partner websites, utilizing targeted SEO and PPC strategies to attract high-quality prospects. Our marketing experts ensure that every lead is relevant and valuable to your business.

Step 2: Lead Verification

Our verification process includes

Contact Detail Validation: Ensuring the accuracy of phone numbers and email addresses.

Consent Confirmation: Making sure leads are ready to be contacted.

Prequalification Checks: Verifying budget, asking price, property type, any other realtor involvement and interest level.

Step 3: Appointment Setting & Live Transfers

Once a lead is verified, our account executives connect with potential clients and schedule appointments based on your availability. We also offer live call transfers to ensure seamless communication between you and your prospects.

Step 4: Lead Distribution

Leads are distributed using a fair and efficient system exclusively within your chosen area. You will receive notifications via text and email, and you have the option to decline leads that do not match your criteria.

Step 5: Concierge Service

Our dedicated account executives support you through every step of the process, from nurturing leads to closing deals. We provide strategic advice and continuous support to help you maximize your success.
